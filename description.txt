Pets Overhaul turns all of your solely cosmetic purposed Pets to directly impact and enhance your gameplay with unique, interesting, build-defining effects and abilities for every situation there is!

Weekly updates!

Features
Explore 65+ complex Pet Effects, all with unique benefits that fits the Pets personality. Pick whichever Pet meets your needs the most!
10 new Light Pet effects, with a unique Quality mechanic for their stats. Will you be lucky enough to obtain a perfect 100% Quality Light Pet?
10+ Town Pet buffs obtained by "petting" the Town Pets.
Pet Tamer Town NPC, selling Pets Overhaul related items and combines your Light Pets.
44 Achievements, related to Pets and their effects, even including some hidden easter eggs!
+10 Custom Items, mostly for recipe purposes.
Added Crafting Recipes for every single Pet (Expensive & difficult recipes, acting as alternate method of obtaining)
Active Regular and Active Light Pet slots to be able to place a Pet Item in to benefit from them, without them appearing on world.

Use /pet command to see all Pets Overhaul commands in-game. ("/pet" = help command)
Example: "/pet class all" will show all Pets affected by Pets Overhaul, or "/pet class offensive" will show all Pets classified as Offensive class. 

Pets Overhaul currently supports Simplified Chinese, Russian and Brazilian Portuguese besides English for localization options. (See below for credits)
